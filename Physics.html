Class 10 Physics Numericals

Key Questions for Bihar Board Syllabus

Chapter 1: Light – Reflection & Refraction

Question 1

A concave mirror forms an image at 20 cm from the mirror when an object is placed at 30 cm. What is the focal length of the mirror?

Question 2

An object is placed 10 cm in front of a convex mirror of focal length 15 cm. Find the image distance and nature of the image.

Question 3

A ray of light enters from air to glass (refractive index = 1.5) at an angle of 30°. Find the angle of refraction.

Question 4

The refractive index of water is 1.33. Find the speed of light in water. (Speed of light in vacuum = 3 × 108 m/s)

Question 5

An object is placed 12 cm from a concave mirror of focal length 8 cm. Find the position and nature of the image.

Chapter 2: The Human Eye & Colourful World

Question 6

A person can read a book at 100 cm but not at 25 cm. What power of lens is needed to correct the vision?

Question 7

The far point of a myopic person is 80 cm. What power of lens is required to correct his vision?

Question 8

The focal length of a convex lens is 25 cm. What is its power?

Question 9

A prism causes a deviation of 5° in a light ray. If the angle of incidence is 40° and angle of emergence is 45°, find the angle of prism.

Question 10

A ray of light travels from air (n=1) to a medium of refractive index 1.5. What is the critical angle?

Chapter 3: Electricity

Question 11

A bulb draws 0.5 A current when connected to 220 V. What is its resistance?

Question 12

Calculate the current flowing through a 10 Ω resistor when a potential difference of 5 V is applied.

Question 13

Find the equivalent resistance when three resistors of 2 Ω, 4 Ω, and 6 Ω are connected in series.

Question 14

Find the equivalent resistance when the same resistors (2 Ω, 4 Ω, 6 Ω) are connected in parallel.

Question 15

An electric heater is rated 1000 W, 220 V. Calculate the current drawn by the heater.

Question 16

How much energy is consumed if a 60 W bulb is used for 5 hours?

Question 17

A wire of resistance 10 Ω is doubled in length. What will be the new resistance?

Question 18

A current of 3 A flows through a resistor for 2 hours. How much charge passes through the resistor?

Question 19

A device consumes 500 J of energy in 10 seconds. Find its power.

Question 20

A circuit has a battery of 12 V and two resistors 4 Ω and 6 Ω in series. Find the total current.
